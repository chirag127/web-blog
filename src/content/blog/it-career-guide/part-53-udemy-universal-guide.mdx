> [!IMPORTANT]
> **What does it mean to get rich with minimum risk?**
> - **Zero or low capital requirement:** You invest your *skills* and *time* rather than borrowing money or risking your life savings.
> - **Asymmetrical upside:** The downside is capped at the cost of a Udemy course and a few hours of study, while the upside is a six-figure remote salary, high-ticket freelance retainers, or compound investment growth.
> - **Career & tool leverage:** Learning how to negotiate, write high-conversion copy, automate tasks with AI, or manage index funds.

> [!NOTE]
> **"I am interested in everything. Where do I start?"**
>
> If you have the ambition to become a true polymath (an *Omnimath*)—someone who understands backend systems, reads financial balance sheets, edits video, negotiates like a CEO, and plays the piano—you need a sequence. 
>
> You cannot learn 100 things simultaneously. But you *can* learn 100 things in a precise, interleaved order where each skill acts as a force multiplier for the next. 

---

## The Master Sequence

This is the exact sequence to take 100 courses. It is designed to prevent burnout by interleaving high-logic subjects (programming/math) with high-creativity subjects (design/music) and high-leverage business skills.

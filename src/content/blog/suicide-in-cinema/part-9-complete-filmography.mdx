## How to Find These Films

Most of these titles are available on major streaming platforms:
- **Netflix**: 13 Reasons Why, BoJack Horseman, After Life, Maniac, The Perks of Being a Wallflower
- **Amazon Prime**: Fleabag, Suicide Club, Shoplifters
- **HBO/Max**: Euphoria, The Hours
- **Apple TV+**: The Crowded Room, The Bridge
- **YouTube**: Some older titles available for free or rent
- **Disney+ Hotstar**: Select Bollywood titles

*Availability varies by region. Check JustWatch.com for current streaming options in your country.*
